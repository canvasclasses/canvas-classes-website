A product launch needs **Problem → Agitate → Solution (PAS)** stretched across the quarter:

**The #1 infrastructure requirement (non-negotiable):** a single owned **lead-capture destination** live before Jul 3 — a "Canvas Vision / Founding Members" waitlist (email + WhatsApp/Telegram) on canvasclasses.in. Every video CTA points there. **Views ≠ funnel. If we are not capturing emails/numbers from Day 3, July is wasted.** Target: a meaningful warm list by Aug 31 to launch into.

3. **Anchor each statement in a truth parents already suspect** → it reads as *validation*, not provocation.
