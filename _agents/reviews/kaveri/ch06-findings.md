The single biggest systemic weakness is **assessment integrity in the difficulty-3 "inference/distractor" quizzes**: a recurring pattern where the correct option is the *most complete/most thematically-worded* one and the distractors are throwaways, plus several "open" reasoning prompts whose `reveal` over-asserts certainty the text doesn't license (e.g. the friends' religions, "tears of pride"). The chapter is also long (18 pages) with heavy cross-page repetition of the violin/Europe fact and the play-vs-poem "twin melody" framing — it reads slightly machine-stamped by the end.
