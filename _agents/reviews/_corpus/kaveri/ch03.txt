--- block #9 [text] id=a428d806-92de-405c-94ee-f1554773f3f9 ---
{
 "markdown": "Look at these from the article: *pankhas **have become** craft items; women workers **have worked** tirelessly; tribes **have adopted** this handicraft; the craft **has transformed** into a business.*\n\nThese verbs describe the **past** — but with **relevance to NOW**.
